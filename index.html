Ben D'Alessandro

Background

The goat was born in San Francisco in 2008. He knew from a young age he was a genius as he begged his parents to take him to the contrustion sites. Soon, he wasnt the only child as he gained a brother, best friends and a pal!

Now

Ben has been a Westtown Staple as you can always find him in the corner with tissues with the answers to all your physics problems. Unlike others, Ben knows the struggle of physics but most of all understands how to get back on the top of the food chain. Ben is him.

Future

As a incoming Dukie, he will be double majoring in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science. He will be the future we have all been waiting for.
